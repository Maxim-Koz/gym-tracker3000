Privacy Policy for Reptr

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Introduction

Reptr respects your privacy. This privacy policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and how it is protected when you use the Reptr application.

Information We Collect

When you create an account, we may collect your email address. We also store workout information that you choose to record, such as exercises, sets, repetitions, and workout history.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide the app's features, including creating your account, saving your workouts, and displaying your progress.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase services. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. Your information is only used to provide and maintain the application.

Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at: minimsgymtracker@gmail.com

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
